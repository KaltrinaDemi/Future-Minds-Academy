Butterflies are fascinating insects known for their vibrant colors and graceful flight. Here are some interesting facts about butterflies: Metamorphosis: Butterflies undergo a remarkable transformation known as metamorphosis. They start as eggs, hatch into caterpillars, then form pupae (chrysalises), and finally emerge as beautiful adult butterflies. Variety: There are thousands of butterfly species worldwide, each with its unique colors and patterns. Some of the most well-known include the Monarch, Swallowtail, and Painted Lady.
